NUTRILOG — FINAL NETLIFY UPLOAD
================================

UPLOAD THIS FOLDER ONLY:
  C:\Users\user\Projects\nutrilog-deploy\dist

HOW:
  1. Netlify → your site → Deploys
  2. Drag the "dist" folder into "Deploy manually"
  OR go to https://app.netlify.com/drop

  Same Netlify link — replaces the live site.

AFTER UPLOAD:
  Close the site tab on your phone and open again (fresh load).

SIGN IN (Settings → Account):
  - First name
  - Email
  - Password
  - Create account  OR  Sign in

SUPABASE (one-time, in supabase.com dashboard):
  1. Authentication → URL Configuration
     Site URL = your Netlify link
     Redirect URLs = https://YOUR-SITE.netlify.app/**
  2. Authentication → Providers → Email
     Turn OFF "Confirm email" for easiest sign-up (optional)
